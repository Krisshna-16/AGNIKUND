Before You Walk In

Questions, answered straight.

Clear facts, no marketing fluff. Everything you need to know before stepping onto the floor.

Do I need prior experience? +

No. Every program scales from absolute beginner to competitive athlete. Our coaches adapt the movements to match your current physical capacity.

Is there a trial class? +

Yes. You can book a free introductory session using the action button below to test our equipment, meet the crew, and experience a workout.

What should I bring on day one? +

Bring comfortable training apparel, clean indoor athletic shoes, and water. We supply fresh workout towels and hydration refill stations.

Is Zumba included in every membership? +

No. Zumba and other group classes are exclusive to Pro and Elite tiers. The Basic tier is dedicated purely to independent gym floor training.

Can I switch between programs? +

Yes. Your training focus can evolve. You are free to pivot between Strength, Conditioning, and Combat tracks in coordination with your coach.

What if I miss a scheduled class? +

You can catch up on missed sessions during open gym hours or reschedule your slot. Inform your coach ahead of time to coordinate.
